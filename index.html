About Me
I'm Nicci and I'm from the wilderness, also known as the Upper Peninsula of Michigan. There I fostered my love of music, or more specifically, musical theater. As most young adults do, I soon realized that my first love was not meant to last. I've had a successful career since then, first as an Accounting Director, and then as a Risk Management Director for a major retailer. Along the way I've trained as a medical transcriptionist and become a certified investigative interrogator. None of these, however, held the magic mix of fulfillment, work-life balance, and sustainability.

I have a unique blend of personal characteristics, thorough training, and life experience to bring the best of the best to your business. Whether it's the ever-elusive eye-catching landing page or a seamlessly cross-platform interface that you prioritize, I'm prepared to over-deliver.
